DETAILS

We are holding our ceremony and reception at Goward House, located at 2495 Arbutus Road in Victoria BC. Please join us at 4:00 PM to allow time for you to get comfortable and find a seat before the ceremony begins at 4:30 PM. Please follow the signs to the back garden of the property.

Following the ceremony, there will be time to visit and we will be offering light refreshments and games during our casual “cocktail hour”. You are welcome to spend time indoors or outdoors, and there will be plenty of seating on the second floor patio and inside in the second floor parlour rooms.

Things to note:
  • Dress code: casual! We want you to be comfortable. Keep in mind it may be warm and sunny during the day. We will be indoors from dinner onward.
  • Unfortunately, we are only able to accommodate children in the wedding party at our ceremony and reception.

ABOUT US

We met in July of 2013 and started dating soon after. While Jeff was at school and Arynne was working odd hours, we spent almost every evening together watching movies and taking late night walks. We bonded over our similar sense of humour. Arynne won Jeff over with her delicious cooking, and Jeff won Arynne over by helping her find her misplaced items... and making up for her complete lack of directional sense.

In May of 2017 we moved in together and have quickly built a life together, enjoying camping, travelling around the island, and spending time with each other’s families and friends. Jeff proposed to Arynne in February 2019 at our favourite place (home!) after cooking Arynne her favourite meal.

We are so excited for the next chapter of our life together and can’t wait to celebrate with those we love.
