LOCATION

Goward House

Goward House is a 2-storey building located at 2495 Arbutus Road. Accessible entrances to both floors are located on the outside of the building – we will provide signage with directions on site. Please note there is no smoking allowed on Goward House property, and any smoking must occur at the edge of the property on Arbutus Road.

TRANSPORTATION

Drive, Bus, Cab, Bike!

You can reach Goward House by car via Cadboro Bay Road from downtown Victoria. Please use the map below for directions as needed. Parking is available onsite, and cars can be left overnight and picked up the next morning. We encourage guests to bus, bike, or carpool if practical. If you need any help getting home, please let us know – the safety of our guests is very important to us!

The number 11 bus stops right in front of venue. It runs every 15 minutes during the day, and every half hour during the evening, with buses running through the end of our reception. If you want to take a taxi home you can call Bluebird 250-382-2222 or Yellow Cab at 250-381-2222.
